Throughout my Special Olympics coaching journey, I've had the privilege of training over 300 coaches and 2000 special athletes, along with 1000 unified partners in Sri Lanka. As a dedicated volunteer since 2015, I've participated in national tournaments as the Swimming coach and underwent international training for intellectually disabled sports. Handling children with different abilities, including blind athletes, has been a transformative experience. Among my proudest achievements, I coached a blind child to swim and empowered a hyperactive 8-year-old to excel in the water, reaffirming the value of inclusivity and the boundless potential of every athlete.

Certifications:

  • Special Olympics Unified Sports Coaching Course - 2023
  • Unfied Sports for Special Olympics Youth Leaders - 2023
  • Training for Sports Performance - Singapore - 2014
